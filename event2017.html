  • Talk: Network Design with Parametric Uncertainties

    • India has by has developed an infrastructure that springs the nation as one of the strong economies in the modern world. An immediate approach to address aforementioned problems is to find optimal solutions within the context of concerned systems. Dr Bharadwaj had discussed both the aforementioned issues : how to model uncertainty in robust optimization problems and furthermore how to find fast exact optimal solutions utilising geometrical and combinatorial structure of the problem in context.
    Date: 18/12/2017
    Speaker:Dr. Avinash Bhardwaj from Center for Operations Research and Econometrics, UC Louvain, Belgium

  • Talk: Environmental Sustainability – Myth OR Reality?

    • Pollution from anthropogenic sources has led to widespread environmental degradation. Development, which is environmentally unsustainable, has potential to threaten the very existence of the human race on this planet earth. As a result, man must learn to live in an environmentally sustainable way in order to limit the widespread damage to environment.
    Date: 14/12/2017
    Speaker:Prof. Ajit P. Annachhatre
